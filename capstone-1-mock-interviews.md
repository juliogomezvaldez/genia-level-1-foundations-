# Mock Interviews – Fact-Checking Auditor (Single-Agent)

## Senior prompts (with aggressive follow-ups)
1. Explain your architecture end-to-end. Where does it fail first?
   - Follow-up: Show the exact contracts between agents/tools.
2. How do you prevent hallucinations?
   - Follow-up: How do you enforce “evidence-first” outputs?
3. What would you do if retrieval returns conflicting sources?
   - Follow-up: How do you decide what’s authoritative?
4. How do you measure quality?
   - Follow-up: Show your golden set and regression strategy.
5. What are your cost/latency controls?
   - Follow-up: What do you drop first under budget pressure?
